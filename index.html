Solving quadratic equation

\[ ax^2+bx+c=0 \\ x = \frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\]

Solving quadratic equation with R

solvequad <- function(a,b,c){
    (-b+c(1,-1)*(b^2-4*a*c)^0.5)/(2*a)
}
solvequad(1,5,6)
